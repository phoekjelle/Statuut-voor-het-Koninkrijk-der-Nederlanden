All persons within the United States, or otherwise subject to its jurisdiction or that of any State, shall be equal before the law and shall have the right to equal protection and benefit of the law.

The state may not unfairly discriminate directly or indirectly against anyone on one
or more grounds, including race, gender, sex, pregnancy, marital status, ethnic or
